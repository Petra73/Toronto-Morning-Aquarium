Morning Aquarium is an emerging aquarium that combines play, dining and shopping.

It is only 15 minutes drive from the city center, especially great for friends, families and couples to come and play.

Here, you can enjoy a variety of services, colorful activities, and excellent shows.

Our goal is to make everyone happy here.
